Why a guesthouse

Why this website has born? Who is it for? What is a Guesthouse? We get often asked these questions from users and obviously not everyone knows what one really mean talking about a Guesthouse. The idea of this website has born after some travels in Indonesia during the early 2000s, to Bali, the Island of the Gods, the ideal destination for a vacancy in the broad sense, where culture and religion, nature and simple life create a mix of unforgettable atmospheres sought-after travels. From the beginning when we planning a travel, the principal purpose of the most of the people, is to saving money for the overnight, and then choose to stay in the countless low-cost Guesthouses in the area, which objectively have a good price/quality ratio, which change, as usual, according to the location, the closeness of the infrastructures, and the services offered. By using the term ‘Guesthouse’ we intend to indicate an accommodation comparable to a hostel, a small hotel, a Bed & Breakfast or Homestay, which peculiarity is to have a low-average cost, and which offers basic rooms (low-cost) with toilet, and in some cases also a balcony, with a family-run business or not. In many cases, in the locations which had become the most famous tourist attractions, the Guesthouses in the area were nothing more than private household that the locals decided to convert to actual lodging houses to sleep, in response to the strong tourist demand, let’s think of the Cuban “Casa Particular”, or the “Studios” throughout Greece. No wonder if you can find guesthouses in the most sought-after tourist attractions, in particular the highly frequented areas during the whole year, and surprisingly, we can find also guesthouses on the Maldivian atolls, the African forests, in the Seychelles paradise and so on. Many owners put their passion and dedication in this “conversion” of their home and this spread positively to the guest. These accommodations allow the guests to accommodate at reasonable prices compared to the other more expensive solutions, but still having the standard services of a normal tourist facility, such as, room cleaning, assistance, breakfast included, and also more attention to the customer compared with a hotel. However, sometime the restricted budget may involve some differences in the expectation of a hotel: for example, while the latter usually has a 24/7 and seven days a week staff assistance, guesthouses have a much more limited staff attendance and therefore sometimes this can implicate doing the check-in by appointment only or in specific time slot. There are different services offered: many guesthouses offer their homemade cooking so the guests can taste the typical local products and lot of the times they can eat together with the other fellow dinner which are in the other accommodations, creating a sort of communitarian and joyful atmosphere, bringing the guest in the local daily life staying strongly in contact with the local life, the tastes, habits, embarking a journey within a journey, having very different experiences compared to the ones they could have in the tourist resorts or in the Hotels; it is like touching with your hand the everyday reality, sharing experiences by having a closer view of the customs and traditions of the country and the people, making them happy to host and greet the travellers. Thus, on this website, among the many options, we propose Guesthouses with common kitchen which can be useful for saving money while eating (by avoiding to eat in a restaurant which is certainly much more expensive) and are also a way to make new friends and eat all together. There are others with a laundry room, private or shared bathroom and private parking for motor-scooters; they can offer a shared swimming pool and Wi-Fi internet connection. Others can benefit of a spectacular view of the sea or the mountain, lake or river and in some cases even the Volcano view. Guesthouses have different targets: university students with a limited budget, families with 4/5 members who wants to strongly reduce their expenses preferring this kind of accommodations; backpackers that want to limit the costs to afford a long time travel and couples who prefer the “familiar” simplicity and tranquillity instead than a hotel solution. Summarizing, there are a lot of benefits in staying in a Guesthouse:


• Affordable price • Extremely informal
• Possibility to share some time with the local family
• Many of these are near the tourist locations, so they are convenient as a base from which you can go to explore the area
• If you are not satisfied about the place, after all you’ve just waste some dollars.
