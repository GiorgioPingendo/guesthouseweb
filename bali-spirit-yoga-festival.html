Bali Spirit Yoga Festival in Ubud - Celebration of Spirit

Ubud has become the main destination for those seeking the deepest soul of Bali, amid spirituality and harmony, culture and local cooking lessons, yoga, pilates and dance, in search of psycho-physics wellness.
It is just here that, for nine consecutive years, will take place one of the major event in the world attracting each year more and more fans from the entire globe that is, the Bali Spirit Yoga Festival.

From 29 March to 3 April 2016 Ubud will be one more time invaded by thousands of people coming from everywhere to follow one of the biggest event in the world dedicated to spirituality, yoga and all the olistic disciplines which nourish the soul in order to find inspiration and increase your own EGO and share it in harmony with others all sourronded till late in the night with the constant presence of music, made of rythm and vitality which play an important role for your own soul.
Only a characteristic is to be required: go there with a smile, open your hearth and soul and share your own knowledge and positive energies with the others. Workshops and seminars will follow between Ubud and its neighbours as well as multi-level lessons of yoga in all its forms that is, Acro Yoga, Yin Yoga, Mantra, Kundalini, Hata, Tantra and many others, dancing lessons such as Capoeira, African Dance, Ecstatic Dance, lessons on various form of respiration and meditation. The greatest experts coming from all over the world will take part. Yogi, dancers, world-class musicians will meet at Bali Spirit Festival to join these five days in the name of spiritual union instead of cultural diversity.
As concerns music, the first two night (Wednesday/Thursday) will be in detail dedicated to Bhakti Nights being accompanied with sacred music and songs as well as the so-called delirious Ecstatic Dance, which is a mixture of dance and free movement to express yourself at best without limits.

The last three-days weekend (Friday/Saturday/Sunday) will be characterized by One World One Stage, a series of concerts that combine genres from all over the world such as folk and electronic music with traditional and modern dance as well as physical theater in two different stages.
A wonderful, world-famous, many colored and spiritual event for thousands of people fond of olistic disciplines and not only and keen on the inspiration that Bali gives each time you experience it. A bit of history: the event was born in 2008 by the founders who decided to join their economic resources in order to create an ecologic and spiritual event making Bali a renowed turistic destination again after the drop in tourism caused by the terroristic attacks of 2002 and 2005. Undoubtely the target has been attained since about 6.000 smiling people are being estimated to take part to Bali Spirit Festival this year too.
