UBUD FOOD FESTIVAL

Ubud is a must destination of the island of Bali especially for those in search of the deepest and relaxed atmosphere where tourists can choose between lessons of pilates, Yoga and all the olistic disciplines as well as lessons of Balinese and Indonesian cooking. There are plenty of restaurants and tourist accomodations such as family-farming guesthouses and homestay organizing cooking lessons capable of attracting more and more tourists; often the lessons are carried out in the morning when you can visit the market that exposes the local products such as fruit, vegetables, meat, spices and each kind of scents used in the local traditional cuisine as well as cooking lessons with the realization of recipes by the chef himself and tasting by the guests. By combining the passion for the kitchen and her love for Bali, the Australian Janet De Neefe who on the island married to a Balinese, has opened guesthouses and restaurants and created the literary festival Ubud Writers & Readers Festival as well as another ambitious project named the Ubud Food Festival. Born in 2015, just from its first edition it proved to be enormously successful by attracting the presence of about 6.500 fans and employees coming from everywhere to promote the culture, the essence of food and the Indonesian autochtonous products confering them, in some cases, an international importance. The three days of this exicting cooking adventure (from 27 to 29 May 2016) foresee cooking lessons, workshops, arguments about local food, forum about products of biological cultivations, book announcement parties, movies, eno-gastronomic tasting and many other. You are going to know the typical products of the island of Bali and not only such as red rice, Asian vegetables, tempeh, honey, coconut sugar and the varied and very perfumed spices as well as the typical Balinese dishes which inspired many world-class chefs who review them in their own way. Ubud Food Festival can be a perfect pretext to visit Bali and know the city of Ubud that continually offers cultural and gastronomic activities and you will be carried away during the three days of the event into its varied culinary genuine world. All we can do is to wish you Good Appetite!
